Case Study: College Library Management System

A college library requires a database system to manage its daily operations efficiently. Book records, student details, and issue/return records are currently maintained manually, resulting in data redundancy, difficulty tracking issued books, search delays, and errors in fine calculation.
Objectives of the System
  • Store and retrieve book details efficiently
  • Maintain student information and membership records
  • Track book issue and return transactions accurately
  • Automatically calculate fines for overdue returns
  • Manage librarian records and responsibilities
  • Ensure data consistency, integrity, and easy retrieval
